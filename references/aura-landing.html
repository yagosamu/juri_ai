The Philippines’ AI Rulebook: Laws, Guidelines & What’s Next

BA
By BAAND Law Research

"Artificial Intelligence is no longer a futuristic concept — it is embedded in our everyday lives. This article provides a baseline for building lawful AI systems."

The regulatory landscape for Artificial Intelligence in the Philippines is rapidly evolving. For organizations, innovators, and compliance teams, understanding the intersection of existing laws and emerging guidelines is critical.

Data Privacy & The DPA

The core privacy law that applies to AI right now is the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173). NPC Advisory 2024-04 reminds controllers to ensure accuracy, fairness, transparency, and purpose limitation when processing data through AI workflows.

Key Obligations for PICs

  • Ensure lawful basis for processing personal data.
  • Uphold transparency and data subject rights.
  • Conduct a Privacy Impact Assessment (PIA).

E-Commerce & Consumer Protection

AI-enabled platforms face heightened obligations under the Internet Transactions Act of 2023 (RA 11967). Chatbots and recommendation engines must be fair, accurate, and explainable to avoid deceptive practices.
